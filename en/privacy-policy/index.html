Privacy Policy – SCOOPS MERDOLF FARM

Last updated: September 15, 2025

1. Data Collection

SCOOPS MERDOLF FARM collects only the data necessary for business relations:

  • Name and surname
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Type of request (farming, fish farming, agriculture)

2. Purpose

This data is used exclusively for:

  • Responding to your inquiries
  • Managing your orders
  • Sending you information about our products (if you are subscribed to the newsletter)

3. Data Sharing

No personal data is sold or shared with third parties. Only members of the SCOOPS MERDOLF FARM team have access to this information.

4. Data Retention Period

Data is retained for 12 months after the last interaction, unless deletion is requested.

5. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Request its correction
  • Request its deletion

Contact us at: Merdolffarm@gmail.com

6. Security

We implement technical and organizational measures to protect your data against loss, unauthorized access, or modification.
