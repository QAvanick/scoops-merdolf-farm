Terms and Conditions of Sale – SCOOPS MERDOLF FARM

Last updated: September 15, 2025

1. Scope

These General Terms and Conditions of Sale apply to all product sales by SCOOPS MERDOLF FARM, an agricultural cooperative based in Meyo, Yaoundé, Cameroon, specializing in pig farming, fish farming, and crop production.

2. Products Offered

We sell:

  • Live pigs or slaughtered pork (on request)
  • Fresh fish (tilapia, carp, etc.)
  • Agricultural products (vegetables, cassava, maize, etc.)

3. Prices and Payment

Prices are listed in XAF (Central African Francs). Payment can be made in cash, bank transfer, or mobile money (MTN Mobile Money, Orange Money). No goods will be delivered without prior payment.

4. Orders and Availability

All orders must be confirmed in writing (via WhatsApp, email, or SMS). Product availability depends on the production cycle. In case of unavailability, the customer will be notified within 24 hours.

5. Delivery

Delivery is available in Yaoundé and surrounding areas. Shipping costs are borne by the customer unless otherwise agreed. Pickup at our location in Meyo is also possible.

6. Complaints

Any dispute regarding product quality must be reported within 24 hours of receipt. We commit to finding a fair solution.
