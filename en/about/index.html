Our Story

A cooperative farm serving Cameroon

Founded on March 20, 2022, SCOOPS MERDOLF FARM is a Simplified Cooperative Society (SCOOPS) registered under number RCC/22/035/CMR/CE/02/010/COS/002003/0020 and identified by NIU M032217277422L.

We began operations in Meyo, Essabi, Awae district, Yaoundé, with a clear mission: to produce safe, local and sustainable food to strengthen national food security.

Today, from our headquarters located in Kondengui, Yaoundé IV, we develop modern, integrated and responsible agriculture — pigs, fish and crops — to feed Cameroonian families.
